All about Dandelions

"Though dandelion today is considered by most people to be a useless weed, in truth it is one of the most beneficial and healthful of herbs. Every part of the dandelion has a use, ranging from food, to medicine, to dye. These beneficial properties did not always go unnoticed in North America: Up until the 1800s, people would actually pull grass out of their yards to make room for dandelions and other useful "weeds" such as chickweed, malva, and chamomile."
